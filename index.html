About Us

Founded in 2005, Greenfield Academy prepares students for a changing world by combining strong academics with social-emotional learning, arts and sports.

Mission

To develop curious, compassionate and capable learners who contribute positively to their communities.

Vision

Every student leaves Greenfield equipped to solve problems, collaborate and continue learning for life.

Values

  • Respect & Inclusion
  • Academic Excellence
  • Creativity & Inquiry

Programs & Curriculum

We offer a balanced curriculum with strong emphasis on STEM, languages, arts, and physical education.

Early Years

Play-based learning, literacy foundations and exploration.

Primary

Integrated subjects, project-based units, and formative assessment.

Secondary

Rigorous discipline-based courses, electives and university preparation.

Clubs & Enrichment

Robotics, debating, visual arts, music, community service and sports teams.
